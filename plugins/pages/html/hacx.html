In HacX the player must take charge of one-time master hacker, turned cyberspace solider of fortune Danny Evanger. Your mission, should you want the paycheck, is to infiltrate the databases of the Genemp Corporation and bring an end to the global chaos brought forth by their experimental biological abominations.

Originally released in 1997 as a total conversion for id Software's DOOM II, HacX was generally unsuccessful. It was later re-released and distributed for free by a former developer. HacX has since received much praise among the DOOM community, in particular for it's impressive artwork.
