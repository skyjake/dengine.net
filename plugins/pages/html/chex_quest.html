Strange slime creatures known as the Flemoids are mercilessly kidnapping the peaceful folk of Bazoik. Assuming the role of Chex Man, elite warrior of Chex Squadron, the player must take on these alien invaders and hopefully save his fellow citizens.

Originally released in 1996 as a pack-in promotion with boxes of Chex cereal.
