Such add-ons include high-resolution graphics, audio packs and even 3D models of your favorite nightmare creatures. Here you will find a collection of some our own favorite add-on packages.

Please refer to the wiki for generic add-on installation instructions (some may require further configuration -- see the add-on's README or other documentation).
